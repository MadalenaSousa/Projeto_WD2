Pamela Card Rome inspired collection presented in Uffizi Museum in Florence

18 December 2024

Renowned jewelry designer Pamela Card has unveiled her highly anticipated collection, drawing inspiration from the timeless beauty of Rome, in a stunning presentation at the prestigious Uffizi Museum in Florence. The event, which took place last week, brought together art, history, and fashion in a setting as rich in cultural heritage as the collection itself.

“Honored italian art and culture and transformed its beauty into metal”

Jonh Smith Maria Doe

The collection, which blends classical Roman aesthetics with modern design, celebrates the grandeur and intricate details of Rome’s ancient architecture, sculptures, and iconic landmarks. Each piece is a tribute to the Eternal City, infused with the spirit of its storied past and the elegance of its present. From the majestic columns of the Colosseum to the delicate patterns of Roman mosaics, Pamela Card’s designs reflect her deep connection to the city’s artistic and architectural legacy. “Rome has always held a special place in my heart,” said Pamela Card at the event. “The city’s layers of history, its art, and its timeless allure have been a constant source of inspiration for my work. This collection is a way to honor that, while reinterpreting these elements through a contemporary lens.”
